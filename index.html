Emotional Regulation Strategies

Use these science-backed tools to feel calmer, more in control, and better connected to yourself.

Download PDF Checklist

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Notice 5 things you can see, 4 touch, 3 hear, 2 smell, 1 taste.

Box Breathing

Breathe in 4s, hold 4s, out 4s, hold 4s.

Movement

Stretch, dance, jump to release energy.

Cold Splash

Cold water on face/wrists resets your system.

Name It

Say or write your feelings aloud.

Textured Touch

Hold soft or bumpy items like fidget toys.

Push/Hug

Push hands together or hug a pillow.

Safe Place Visualization

Imagine a calm place in detail.

Body Scan

Focus from toes to head, releasing tension.

Affirmations

Say: I am safe. I am enough.

Music

Listen to music and focus on sounds.

Color Hunt

Look for everything blue (or another color) around you.
